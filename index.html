The Hygge Project

Hygge Student Cinema is a platform that showcases the creativity and talent of up-and-coming filmmakers from around the world. Whether you're a film buff or just looking for a night out, student cinema offers a diverse range of films that are sure to entertain and inspire.



Cultural Awareness

Cultural awareness is a central pillar of Hygge's mission, as we believe that exposure to diverse films and cultures is essential to promoting understanding and empathy. By screening films from different countries and cultures, we aim to broaden our audience's perspectives and encourage them to engage with and appreciate different societies and lifestyles.

Community Building

Community building is another important pillar of Hygge's mission. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where students from different faculties and backgrounds can come together to socialize and connect. Through our cinema events, we aim to foster a sense of community and belonging among high school students, helping them to build meaningful relationships and connections.

Skill Development

Skill development is the third pillar of Hygge's mission. By involving students in the planning and execution of our cinema events, we aim to provide them with practical opportunities to develop essential organizational, leadership, and communication skills. We believe that these skills are valuable not just for future careers, but also for personal growth and development.
