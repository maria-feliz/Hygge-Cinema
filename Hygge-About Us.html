About The Hygge Project

Our Benefits



Promoting Cultural Awareness

A student cinema can serve as a valuable educational tool for promoting cultural awareness and tolerance. By showcasing films from diverse cultures and countries, it offers a unique platform for students to learn about other societies and lifestyles, fostering a greater understanding and empathy towards people from different backgrounds. Exposure to films from various cultures can broaden students' perspectives and challenge their preconceived notions about the world. It allows them to explore different customs, traditions, and ways of life, providing insight into the complexity and diversity of human experience. This not only enriches their cultural understanding but also helps them to appreciate the richness of their own culture and heritage.

Building a Sense of Community

By bringing together people from different faculties and backgrounds, student cinemas can provide a space for students to socialize, make new friends, and build relationships that extend beyond the walls of the cinema. The communal experience of watching a film together can be a powerful bonding experience. Students can share in the emotional journey of the film, react to unexpected plot twists, and discuss their thoughts and opinions after the screening. This shared experience can create a sense of camaraderie and shared understanding among the audience, regardless of their individual backgrounds or beliefs.

Developing Organizational Skills

One important aspect of running a student cinema is scheduling. Cinema organizers must carefully select films to showcase, taking into account factors such as audience interest, cultural relevance, and scheduling conflicts. This requires careful research and coordination with other events and activities on campus. In addition to film selection, student cinema organizers must also manage budgets and finances. This includes developing a budget for each screening, tracking expenses, and seeking out funding or sponsorships to support the cinema's operations. Marketing is another critical aspect of running a student cinema. Organizers must effectively promote each screening, utilizing social media, flyers, and other marketing channels to reach a broad audience. Effective marketing can help to increase attendance and build the cinema's reputation on campus.

Learn more about organizational skills

Improving Public Speaking

Hosting events, introducing films, and leading discussions at a student cinema can be a valuable way for students to improve their public speaking skills. These activities require students to communicate effectively with an audience, express their ideas clearly, and engage with others in a meaningful way. By hosting events, student cinema organizers have the opportunity to practice their public speaking skills in front of a live audience. This can include introducing films, welcoming guests, and providing context and background information about each screening. Effective public speaking can help to build audience engagement and interest, and can help to establish the credibility and authority of the student cinema.

Learn more about public speaking

Offering Career Opportunities

For students interested in film production, a student cinema can provide opportunities to learn about the filmmaking process and gain hands-on experience in various aspects of production. For example, students may have the opportunity to work on short films or video projects, learning about lighting, camera work, sound design, and post-production. This experience can be valuable in building a portfolio and gaining practical skills that can be applied in future film projects.

Learn more about cinema career opportunities

Providing Affordable Entertainment

A student cinema can be an important resource for students who are on a tight budget, as it can offer affordable entertainment options that are not always available through commercial cinemas. With the rising costs of movie tickets, concessions, and transportation, many students may be unable to afford regular trips to commercial cinemas. Student cinemas, however, often offer significantly lower ticket prices, making it more accessible and affordable for students to attend screenings. Moreover, a student cinema can provide an alternative to the mainstream films that are typically shown in commercial cinemas. This is particularly important for students who are interested in independent or foreign films that may not receive widespread distribution. By showcasing a range of films from different cultures and countries, a student cinema can broaden students' cinematic horizons, while also offering an affordable and accessible entertainment option.

Our objectives

Our mission is to provide a safe, effective and enjoyable environment for all students who wish to present passion projects such as illustration, or independent films, and for students to socialize as they support these projects.

Hygge Student Cinema offers a comfortable and accessible way of entertainment for our audience, that is, high school students. Hygge Student Cinema is designed to provide a comfortable and enjoyable viewing experience for students. This includes comfortable seating, high-quality sound systems, and other amenities that make the experience of watching a film more enjoyable.

The overall mission of Hygge Student Cinema is to provide a safe, accessible, and enjoyable entertainment option for high school students. Our aim is to foster a sense of community among students by offering a space where they can come together to enjoy films and engage in discussions. We strive to make our cinema accessible to all students, including those with disabilities or other accessibility needs. Additionally, we aim to provide opportunities for students to develop organizational and communication skills through involvement in the planning and execution of cinema events. Ultimately, our goal is to promote cultural awareness and empathy among students and to provide a valuable and enriching experience for all who attend our cinema.

Our objectives in Hygge Student Cinema include:

1. To provide an engaging and entertaining outlet for students to socialize and enjoy films together.

2. To encourage students to develop critical thinking skills by analyzing and discussing films they watch.

3. To promote cultural awareness by showcasing a diverse selection of films from different countries and backgrounds.

4. To foster teamwork and collaboration among students by involving them in the planning and execution of cinema events.

5. To develop leadership and organizational skills by giving students the opportunity to take on roles such as event coordinator or marketing manager.



Beyond providing an enriching experience for our audience, we also offer practical opportunities for students to gain experience in film production, marketing, and event planning. By involving students in the planning and execution of cinema events, we aim to help them develop essential organizational and leadership skills that can be applied to future careers or personal endeavors.

At Hygge, we are committed to making our cinema accessible to all students, including those with disabilities or other accessibility needs. We offer accommodations such as wheelchair-accessible seating, assistive listening devices, and closed captioning to ensure that all students can fully participate in our cinema events. We also strive to keep ticket prices affordable, making our cinema accessible to students on a tight budget.

Our mission at Hygge (Student Cinema) is to provide a valuable and enriching experience for high school students while promoting cultural awareness, community building, and skill development. We believe that by providing a platform for students to engage with diverse films and cultures, we can help them develop a broader understanding of the world and build empathy towards others. We also hope to inspire students to get involved in their communities and make a positive impact in the world.
